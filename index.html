Who We Are

Forwards Fund is a group of college students eager to help build the world's next biggest companies. We focus on applying first principles problem-solving to college startups in their pre-seed or ideation stages.

We take high risks on people and ideas that we believe have the potential to succeed even when nobody else does. We come alongside founders and offer any form of help we can provide, whether that be funding, feedback, mentorship, or simply a listening ear.

We don't have everything figured out, but we seek to learn and grow with you because there is only one direction to go... forwards.
