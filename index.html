Who We Are

Started by a group of college students passionate about entrepreneurship and innovation, Forwards Fund set out to do whatever it takes to help early-stage founders in their pre-seed or ideation phase to secure venture capital funding and move their dream forwards.

We come alongside founders and offer any form of help we can provide. Our team is experienced across numerous domains including business development, marketing, sales, branding, legal compliance, data analytics, software development, engineering, accounting, finance, and more.

We don't have everything figured out, but we seek to learn and grow with you because there is only one direction to go... forwards.
